awaken your senses

You won’t just see these incredible places. Through special effects you’ll feel them, too.

Engineered to swoop, dip and turn — giving you the true feeling of flight.

Unique scents are used to transport you to the iconic locations in the films for a fully immersive experience.

STOP OVER AT THE LOST CACTUS BAR

Signature cocktails paired with bite-size snacks at The Lost Cactus bar will keep you refreshed before and after your adventure. Slow down and savor the moment or stop by for goodies on the go. With ambient desert breezes, cactus-infused aromatics, and handcrafted delights on the Strip, The Lost Cactus Bar is your basecamp for unforgettable moments—before takeoff and after touchdown.

EMBARK ON A FLAVOR EXPEDITION

Chart a course for bold tastes with our Cactus Bloom Paloma, Spiced Sahara Old Fashioned, and Glacier Breeze Gimlet—each cocktail a tribute to the world’s wildest frontiers. Nibble on Roasted Agave Taquitos, Smoky Chipotle Cactus Fries, and Mini Baja Fish Tacos between sips, or grab a quick snack when you’re racing for your next FlyOver flight.
